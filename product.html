Home Page

After signing up and logging in, the home page will be displayed with all the main features of the application along with the notifications where updates and opportunities are informed to users. The Volunteer signup banner is also presented in the home page so that users interested do not miss out to get themselves enrolled in it.









Food Donation

Beneficiaries can be informed about the consumable period of donated food(especially if it is cooked food) by donors providing essential information such as cooked and expiry date. Aditionally,donors can select appropriate vehicle type to transport food to charity based on quantity of food.









Fund Donation

Donors are presented with various pre-set donation amount options to facilitate a quick and easy donation process. However,they have the flexibility to manually input their desired amount in the provided text field.









Clothes Donation

When making a clothing donation,donors have the option to include photos and a brief note in the description box.These details can include the size of the clothing or any important fabric information.Furthermore,donors can upload photos of the clothing items.If the quantity of clothing exceeds the carrying the carrying capacity of a bike,the donor can opt for the truck transport option.









Books Donation

Donors are encouraged to provide the name of the book(if they are books meant for reading rather than writing) and it's author to aid the beneficiaries in understanding more about the book.Additionally,if the number of books exceeds the carrying capacity of a bike,the donor can choose the truck option for transportation.









Set Pickup Location

Donors choose pickup location for items they are donating.
