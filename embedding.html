About Us

Our Mission

Our food donation app is dedicated to reducing food waste and supporting those in need. We connect donors with local charities and food banks to ensure surplus food reaches those who can benefit from it the most.

How It Works

Our app makes it easy for donors to list surplus food items, which are then picked up by our partner charities. We ensure that the food is safely transported and distributed to those in need.

Meet Our Team

We are a team of five dedicated individuals who are passionate about making a difference. Together, we bring diverse skills and experiences to tackle the issue of food waste and hunger in our community.

Get Involved

We welcome everyone to join our mission. Whether you are an individual looking to donate food, a charity seeking partnerships, or a volunteer wanting to make a difference, we have a place for you in our community.
